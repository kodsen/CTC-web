ABOUT US

Christian Teaching Centre Academy is a vision borne of a ministerial drive to impact this generation, by raising God-fearing and eternity-minded students that are well equipped with all the knowledge and skills which are requisites to advancement in life.

C.T.C Academy was inaugurated on the need to attain academic excellence without compromising the spiritual lives of our learners. Thereby preventing students from earning distinctions and credits at the expense of their souls.

Intergrity, discipline and educational excellence are our watch-words. This is why our teachers and other staff are certified as standard and with sound moral and spiritual backgrounds. They are carefully selected, trusted and armed with the necessary know-how on how to train our students in alignment with the vision of our school.
